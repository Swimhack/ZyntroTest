Shipping & Packaging Guidelines

📦 Packaging Requirements

  • Use leak-proof, sealed containers
  • Include desiccant for moisture-sensitive samples
  • Double-bag liquid samples
  • Use bubble wrap or foam for fragile items
  • Label each sample clearly with unique Sample ID

🚚 Preferred Carriers

  • FedEx: Overnight, 2-day, Ground
  • UPS: Next Day Air, 2nd Day Air
  • USPS: Priority Express, Priority Mail

📋 What to Include

  • Sample inventory list with Sample IDs
